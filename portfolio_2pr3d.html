2 Player Racing 3D

Mobile Unity C# Optimization

Overview

Competitive split‑screen and single‑player mobile racing designed for fast pick‑up‑and‑play sessions. Built in Unity with a focus on responsive driving feel, clean UI, and robust performance on low‑ to mid‑range Android devices.

Key Contributions

  • Custom arcade‑style vehicle physics tuned for touch controls and low input latency.
  • Rendering optimization via draw‑call batching, sprite atlas packing, compressed textures, and pooled VFX.
  • Accessible UI that adapts between single‑player and local two‑player modes.
  • Stable frame times on budget devices; profiled and eliminated GC spikes.

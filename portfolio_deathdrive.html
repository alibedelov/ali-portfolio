Death Drive: Twisted Gears

Arcade racing meets tactical weapon systems and predictive aiming.

Mobile Unity C# Gameplay Systems

Overview

Arcade combat‑racing focused on vehicle‑mounted weapon systems and dynamic targeting mechanics. The project includes a custom turret controller with predictive aim, modular weapon loadouts, and readable combat feedback tuned for mobile screens.

Key Gameplay Systems

  • Turret tracking with rotational smoothing and predictive targeting.
  • Modular weapon loadouts (miniguns, rockets) and progressive upgrades.
  • Collision‑aware driving model tuned for arcade handling.
  • Enemy AI that chases, evades, and disengages based on distance thresholds.
